>About BearCTF

BearCTF is a small CTF, or "Capture the Flag" competition created by high-school senior Jack Herd for his capstone project. It is intended for competitors with some computer science experience, but anyone is welcome to join!

Competitors form teams of 1-3, and answer problems available on the website. Problems consist of a "hacking" challenge where competitors must employ some form of skill, or exploit some vulnerability, to access a "flag". Once the flag is retrieved, it is submitted and the team is awarded points.

The competition begins on the 19th of April, 2016. Users can sign up and form teams before-hand, but cannot access problems. The competition will last for five days, until saturday, April 24.
